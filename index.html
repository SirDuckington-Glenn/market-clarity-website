How It Works

A Systematic Approach to Market Education.

Each morning before the open, the algorithm processes macro data and price structure — and publishes a plain-language educational brief to Telegram. No hype, no calls to action.

01

Macro Environment

Volatility, yields, dollar, gold, and oil are assessed daily to provide educational context on the broader market environment.

02

Price Structure

Opening range levels and key price zones are identified and presented purely for educational and observational purposes.

03

Daily Brief

A structured educational brief is delivered to Telegram — macro context, regime assessment, and rules-based observations. Not advice.

04

Paper Trading Log

A hypothetical paper trading log runs in parallel — demonstrating how rules-based logic processes market data. Purely educational. Not a recommendation.
