What This Is

A Journal, Not a Service.

Every day before the market opens, I document what I observe — macro context, price structure, and the logic my algorithm uses to decide whether conditions are favorable. This is a public record of a process, not a call to action.

01

Daily Macro Observations

Every morning I note what I see in volatility, yields, dollar, gold, and oil — and what it might mean for the broader environment. My personal read, documented.

02

Price Structure Notes

I document the opening range, key levels, and where price is relative to structure. Observation only — not a directive.

03

Algorithm Diary

My rules-based algorithm runs daily. I publish what it sees, what it decides, and why — win or lose. A transparent log of a system in motion.

04

Paper Trading Log

All documented trades are hypothetical paper trades. I record every entry, exit, and outcome — publicly, with no edits. A live trading diary for anyone to follow.
